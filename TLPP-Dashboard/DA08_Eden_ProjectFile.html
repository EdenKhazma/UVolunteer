I. TOP 15 PRIMARY CORE PARTNERS

This list highlights the Top 15 Primary Core Partner library branches. To find the best locations for U+ events, a strategic scoring formula was applied across the Toronto Public Library databases. This formula ranks branches based on two main goals: 60% of the score is based on how their past programs aligned with U+ events, and 40% is based on their capacity (rooms they have for events and their capacity). These 15 branches have the best combination of strong community interest and large rooms, making them a good fit for a launch of multi-session workshops that allignes with U+ objectives. The calculation formula was done based on the max values in each columns (U+ events alignment and capacity), and in 60 to 40 percent in favor of the no. of U+ Alignment events the library is hosting.
